At the start of Combat, roll Initiative: Dexterity + Wits (5-2 dice), Difficulty 6.
Each round of Combat represents 3 seconds of near-simultaneous action.
It's my Turn
  • You get one Movement and one Action every round of Combat.
  • You may Move (Dexterity-Penalty)*5 (35-10) feet for free.
  • You may Dash up to an additional (Brawn*5) (20) feet, but it penalizes your Action -1 die per 5 feet.
  • Action: Attack with your weapon or hands.
  • Action: Sprint by rolling Brawn + Athletics (3-2 dice). Add the Outcome*5 feet to your Movement.
  • Action: Evade by rolling to defend or dodge. Your Outcome is "spent" to reduce the Outcome of any incoming attacks until your next Action.
  • Action: Activate a Power.
  • Hold your Action by declaring an Action and a triggering event. If the event occurs before your next turn, you perform the Action.
I'm being attacked
  • If you have not yet spent your Action this round, you may use it as a Reaction to defend yourself. Otherwise you take the full attack.
  • Reaction: Dodge by rolling Dexterity + Athletics (7-2 dice), Difficulty 7. Your Outcome is subtracted from the attacker's.
  • Reaction: Defend with your weapon by rolling its attack roll, if allowed. Your Outcome is subtracted from the attacker's.
  • Reaction: Clash with the attacker using the same roll you would use to Defend. The higher Outcome is treated as the result of a successful, undefended attack roll against the other.
  • Desperate Defense: If you have already used your Action, you may Exert your Mind to perform a Reaction. Your next round's Action receives a -2 dice penalty.
If an attack's Outcome is positive after defense, it results a new Injury with Severity = Outcome + Weapon damage - Armor
