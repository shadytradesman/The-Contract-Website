At the start of Combat, roll Initiative: Dexterity + Wits (0 dice), Difficulty 6.
Each round of Combat represents 3 seconds of near-simultaneous action.

It's my Turn

You get one Movement and one Action on your turn every Round of Combat.
Movement
  • Movement is taken immediately before OR after your Action. It cannot be split.
  • You may Move (Dexterity + 1 - Penalty)*5 (0) feet for free.
  • You may Dash up to an additional (Brawn*5) (0 feet).
    • Dashing penalizes your Action -1 die per 5 feet.
Actions
  • Attack with your weapon or hands.
  • Sprint by rolling {{tutorial.sprint_roll.render_html_for_current_contractor}}. Add the Outcome*5 feet to your Movement.
  • Evade by rolling to Defend or Dodge. Your Outcome is "spent" to reduce the Outcome of any incoming attacks until your next Action.
  • Activate a Power.
  • Hold your Action by declaring an Action and a triggering event. If the event occurs before your next turn, you perform the Action.

I'm being attacked

If you have not yet spent your Action this Round, you may use it as a Reaction.
Reactions
  • Dodge by rolling {{tutorial.dodge_roll.render_html_for_current_contractor}}. Your Outcome is subtracted from the attacker's.
  • Defend with your weapon by rolling its attack roll. Your Outcome is subtracted from the attacker's. Not always possible (tap/hover for more info).
  • Clash with the attacker using the same roll you would use to Defend. The higher Outcome (not contested) is treated as the result of a successful, undefended attack roll against the other.
Desperate Defense
  • If you have already used your Action, you may Exert your Mind to perform a Reaction. Your next Round's Action receives a -2 dice penalty.
If an attack's Outcome is positive after defense, it results a new Injury with Severity = Outcome + Weapon damage - Armor
