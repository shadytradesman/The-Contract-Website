Proper Stabilization
Requires an advanced first aid kit for Severity 4 Injuries, a surgery setup for Severity 5 Injuries, and a surgery theater for Severity 6+ Injuries.
Roll Intellect + Medicine, Difficulty equal to Severity
  • An Outcome of 1 or greater will Stabilize the Injury and stop its degradation.
  • Outcome 4+ puts the Injury on track to heal clean: its Battle Scar heals with it.
  • On a Botch, the Injury's Severity increases by 1.
  • Failed or Botched attempts may be re-attempted at +1 Difficulty per failure.
