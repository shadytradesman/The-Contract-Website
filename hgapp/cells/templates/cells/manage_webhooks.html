What are web hooks?

Webhooks are an extremely simple way to integrate your World's Discord Server with The Contract's website. They direct bots to post helpful announcement messages in your server when Contracts are scheduled, members join, or World Events occur.

To use them, simply obtain a webhook URL from the channel settings of your Discord server. Paste that URL in one of the forms below, and you're done! The bot will post based on events you have selected.

See Discord's Tutorial for an explanation of how to obtain a webhook URL for your server (it's really easy).
