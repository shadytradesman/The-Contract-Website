This form creates a Game event that helps coordinate running a Game. If you would like to record a completed Game, click here.

Players who you invite or who find your Game on the LFG page may RSVP and declare which Contractors they intend to bring. These Contractors will be revealed to you if they are Private.

When you are ready to start playing, visit your Game's page, click the "Start Game" button, and confirm which Players and Contractors are attending. While the Game is Active, Players will not be able bring their Contractor on other Games.

When the Game is over, transition its status to Finished and declare the outcome for each Contractor. Surviving Contractors will receive Experience and, if they won, Gifts. Those less fortunate are interned in the Graveyard. Regardless of the outcome, the Game's Scenario is revealed to the Players in their Scenario Gallery, and they will be able to view any in-character Journals written about it. As the GM, you can leave feedback about your experience running it.

You are awarded 4 Experience for GMing. If at least one Contractor died and at least one achieves Victory, you are given an Improvement to use on one of your Contractors' Powers.
