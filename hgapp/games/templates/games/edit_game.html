This form schedules an upcoming session that helps coordinate running a Contract. If you would like to record a completed Contract, click here.

Players who you invite or who find your Contract on the LFG page may RSVP and declare which Contractors they intend to bring. These Contractors will be revealed to you if they are Private.

When you are ready to start playing, visit your Contract's page, click the "Start Contract" button, and confirm which Players and Contractors are attending. While the Contract is Active, Players will not be able bring their Contractor on other Contracts.

When the Contract is over, transition its status to Finished and declare the outcome for each Contractor. Surviving Contractors will receive Experience and, if they won, Gifts. Those less fortunate are interned in the Graveyard. Regardless of the outcome, the Contract's Scenario is revealed to the Players in their Scenario Gallery, and they will be able to view any in-character Journals written about it. As the GM, you can leave feedback about your experience running it.

You are awarded 4 Experience for GMing. If any of the Players are brand-new OR at least one Contractor dies and at least one achieves Victory, you are given an Improvement to use on one of your Contractors' Powers.
