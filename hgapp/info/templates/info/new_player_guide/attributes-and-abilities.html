  • Attributes are rated between 1-5.
  • A rating of 2 is the human average. A rating of 1 is below average, and 5 is world-class.
  • Your Attributes' values determine many of your other stats, such as movement speed, Mind (mental fortitude), and Body (physical fortitude).

Abilities define your learned skills and knowledge

  • Abilities are rated between 0 and 5.
  • Even a single rank in an Ability indicates training or experience in that field.Â 
  • Abilities are only used in skill checks. They do not grant stats.
  • You may also invent your own Abilities.
    • These Secondary Abilities must be more specific than the Primary Abilities that appear on all character sheets.
    • In return, you receive a bonus when you use them.
    • Punk's Smuggling Ability is a Secondary Ability; it is unique to her.
    • Secondary Abilities can not grant any supernatural effects.
