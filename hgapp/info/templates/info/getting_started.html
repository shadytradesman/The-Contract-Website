Step 5: Gather your players

At this point, you want to gather your other players, have them make Characters (and hopefully accounts) and get them to join your Cell. They don't have to make accounts or join your Cell, but it makes things easier.

Step 6: Play and Record your Game

Okay. You've got the Stock Scenarios. You know how to GM. Now you can play a Game. There's no need to do anything on the site before you start playing, although you can go through the trouble of using our soon-to-be-drastically-changed Game Scheduler.

The most convenient way to record Games is to simply play them, and then visit your Cell and hit the "Declare a Completed Game" button near the top. That allows you to just say what happened instead of having the other Players accept invites and such.

Once you enter the completed Game, your Players will recieve their Gifts (if they won), and you will receive rewards for GMing. Neat!
